Data deletion

Last updated: 2026-05-29

Truth or Mole accounts

Truth or Mole does not require a Little Brush Games account. Because there is no Little Brush Games account system, there may be no account profile for us to delete.

Support requests

If you contacted us by email, you can request deletion of support messages associated with your email address by contacting support@littlebrushgames.com.

App-store purchases and subscriptions

Purchases, refunds, and subscriptions are managed by the platform where you obtained the app, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. Please use your app-store account settings to manage or cancel subscriptions.

Analytics and technical data

Truth or Mole may use anonymous, aggregated, or pseudonymous analytics and technical diagnostics. Because this data is not intended to identify you by name and may be aggregated, we may not always be able to locate or delete analytics records related to a specific person.

How to make a request

Email support@littlebrushgames.com with the subject “Data deletion request”. Please include the platform you use, such as iOS or Android, and enough information for us to understand the request. Do not send passwords or payment-card details.
